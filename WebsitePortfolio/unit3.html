UNIT 3 Digital Portfolio

This Unit consists of creating a professionally made portfolio, its suppose to show off all the work that you have done and explain all the Units we have studied to show the employer you know what you have studied. There are many subjects which have to be discussed such as the purpose of the portfolio, who is it aimed at, what does the portfolio look like and how it is structured

The main focus of this Unit is to show undestanding and the importance of a portfolio when applying for work, especially in the industry such as IT jobs, Programers, Artists, Animators, etc. Portfolios show off your skills and undestading how to make one is an important skill it self.
